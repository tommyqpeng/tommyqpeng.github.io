About Quantifai

Quantifai is a data-driven technology company focused on building AI tools that empower everyday decision-makers. Our solutions are designed by domain experts, built with cutting-edge methods, and guided by real-world impact.

From consulting case training to environmental analysis tools, our mission is to bring accessible, purposeful, and powerful AI to the hands of users who need it most.

Dr. Tommy Peng is a seasoned data scientist with over a decade of experience in developing AI-enabled data-driven solutions. Tommy has led and delivered over 10 successful data science projects at the University of Auckland, University of Melbourne, and the Bionics Institute, building end-to-end machine learning models and data pipelines that achieved world-leading accuracy.

Tommy’s work has spanned healthcare, construction, and aged-care industries, where he has applied AI to real-world challenges — from improving patient outcomes with millisecond-accurate heartbeat analysis to optimizing construction cost reporting using natural language processing.

He has led publications in academic journals such as Computers in Medicine and IEEE JBHI, and has received multiple national and international awards. As an invited speaker at leading conferences, his ability to translate complex data into actionable insights is widely recognized.

With deep expertise in AI, data pipelines, and model optimization, Tommy is passionate about transforming expert knowledge into purposeful tools that empower better decision-making and create meaningful impact.
